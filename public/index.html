About Us

Gurukul Mahavidyalaya established in village Haliwav. Tehsil-Chitalwana, Dist-Sanchore. It is affiliated with the Jai Narayan Vyas University Jodhpur. We provide excellent graduate education in the faculties of Arts to students, enabling them to serve as mature citizens of society.

The college has been established with a mission to nurture students through humanistic, scientific, and innovative education. Special emphasis is placed on student-centered creative, analytical, and participatory learning. Students are actively involved in various curricular and extracurricular activities to promote overall personality growth.

The college regularly organizes interactions with academicians from different fields to foster the all-round development of students. Excellent infrastructure facilities such as a library, reading room, and outdoor sports courts create a conducive environment for learning.
